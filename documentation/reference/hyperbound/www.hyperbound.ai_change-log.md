# Change Log

February 2025

### Launched Internal Competitions

Teams can now set up internal competitions and track performance across multiple AI bots. The new leaderboard feature allows sales reps to compete and measure progress within a structured, gamified environment.

January 2025

### Added Google Meet & Microsoft Teams Support to Real Call Scoring

Hyperbound now supports Google Meet and Microsoft Teams for real call scoring, expanding beyond Zoom and Gong. This allows teams to automatically analyze and score calls from multiple platforms in one place.

### Revamped Bot Builder UI

The bot-building experience has been redesigned with a more intuitive interface and AI-assisted bot creation. Users can now upload transcripts from real calls to generate bots and receive simplified step-by-step guidance.

December 2024

### Launched Real Call Scoring with Zoom & Gong Integrations

Sales teams can now upload real calls for AI-driven scoring based on predefined criteria. Hyperbound also integrates with Zoom and Gong, allowing calls to be automatically analyzed as they come in.

### Launched AI Meeting Assistant

The new AI Meeting Assistant acts as a call recorder and note-taker, transcribing, summarizing, and scoring calls in real-time. It also generates AI-powered action items, follow-up emails, and shareable links for internal and external collaboration.

November 2024

### Revamped Analytics UI

Analytics now includes 20 new widgets for deeper insights into call performance and training impact. Teams can also export data instantly, making it easier to track and report on rep progress.

### **Introduced Rep Dashboard**

A new rep-level analytics dashboard allows managers to track individual rep performance in detail. The dashboard includes five new widgets to analyze call quality, coaching impact, and skill development over time.

October 2024

### Built Self-Service AI Scorecard Builder

Teams can now create and customize their own AI scorecards without submitting a request. This feature ensures faster, more flexible coaching tailored to each sales process.

### **Revamped Member’s Page UI**

The new members page makes user management easier with bulk actions, enhanced search, and better admin controls. Teams can now invite multiple users at once and quickly manage permissions.

### **Launched Learning Modules**

Hyperbound now allows teams to group AI bots into structured learning paths, track rep progress, and test key skills. This feature also includes blitz-style training sessions for faster skill reinforcement.

September 2024

### Revamped Call History & Call Summary UI

The call review experience has been modernized with interactive scorecards, structured AI coaching feedback, and enhanced filtering. Managers can now analyze performance with more granular insights.

### Revamped Sidebar UI

A cleaner, more intuitive sidebar improves navigation across the platform. Users can now toggle visibility for a more streamlined experience.

### **Launched Video Calls & Screensharing Roleplays**

AI roleplays now support video calls and screen sharing, making sales practice sessions more realistic and engaging.

August 2024

### **Revamped Roleplay UI**

Hyperbound roleplays now have a video-call-style interface with an improved layout for video calls. Users can also see who’s speaking in real time, making interactions more natural and immersive.

### Frequently Asked Questions

#### Can Hyperbound be customized to my company’s sales process?

Yes! Hyperbound supports custom roleplays for any ICP, AI scorecards for any sales methodology or messaging frameworks, and industry-specific objections.

#### How long does set-up and onboarding take?

Building your first bot and scorecard takes less than 10 minutes. Building out your personas and setting up modules and real call score take two weeks on average. Most customers see the value in the first 30 days.

#### What languages does Hyperbound support?

Hyperbound currently supports these 25+ languages, and more upon request: English, Spanish, French / French-Canadian, German, Italian, Polish, Portuguese, Swedish, Romanian, Czech, Danish, Dutch (NL), Chinese (Mandarin), Cantonese (HK), Vietnamese, Japanese, Korean, Hebrew, Gujarati, Hindi, Indonesian, Marathi, Tamil, and Thai.

#### Is Hyperbound an LMS?

No. Hyperbound is an AI sales coach that intelligently analyzes your sales calls and helps your reps practice the scenarios they struggle with the most.

#### Does Hyperbound train on my data?

No! Hyperbound AI does not train on your data. Our models are pre-trained on our own proprietary data sets. Head to our Security Trust Center for more information.

#### Does Hyperbound offer a free trial?

Yes! We have a free interactive demo with a variety of different pre-built AI personas available on our website. Head to our interactive demo to try it yourself. To build your own custom bot, book a demo here.
